About Us

Celestial Bandicoot Bagel Emporium all started cause a couple people just loved some bagels. We wanted to make a whole building dedicated for everything bagel. So thats what we did.

We have our bagel cafetria combining the best types of sandwiches or antying and putting a bagel half on top and one on the bottom. Then we have a little bagel art area where people have just made art out of you guessed, bagels (this also includes art of bagels as well). Also in this area it is filled with facts and history about bagels as well. We want to take you in the full experince about anyrhing bagel at the Celestial Bandicoot Bagel Emporium.
